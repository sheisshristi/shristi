### Content



The ReGenesis IoT Disaster Monitor uses an ESP32-C3 Mini microcontroller as the main processing unit. A DHT22 sensor measures temperature and humidity to detect unsafe environmental conditions. An OLED SSD1306 display shows live data and emergency alerts. A red LED and buzzer provide visual and audio warnings during disasters. Jumper wires and a breadboard are used for circuit connections and prototyping. The system connects to WiFi using the Blynk IoT platform for remote monitoring and alerts. A USB cable powers and programs the device. Wokwi simulator is used for testing and simulation before hardware implementation.
